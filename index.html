The Bhavan stands for the reintegration of Indian Culture.

In a world falling to pieces under the impact of an amoral technological avalanche, it tries to hold fast to the fundamental values for which our culture stands - RITA, SATYA, YAGNA and TAPAS:

  • 1. FAITH in God who informs the Cosmic Order;
  • 2. TRUTH which is accord between mind and word and deed;
  • 3. DEDICATION which offers all moments of life as an offering to God;
  • 4.SUBLIMATION which purifies the body and mind and transmutes instincts, passions and emotions into things of beauty.
